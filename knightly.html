Doctor Keira Knightly

Doctor Knightly was born in London England where she attended Hogwarts Academy. She moved to the United States when she was 18 and enrolled in the College of Veterinary Medicine & Biomedical Sciences at Texas A&M University. She obtained her Ph.D degree in Veterinary Pathobiology and Biomedical Sciences. Doctor Knightly specializes in Exotic animals and joined our facility to handle all of our communities Exotic pets. We are the only animal hospital in northern Michigan to care for Exotic animals.
