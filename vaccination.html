GENERAL SCHEDULE

Most vaccination protocols recommend a series of vaccines for puppies and kittens, with vaccine boosters given at one year of age. Frequency of vaccination thereafter varies depending on the lifestyle of the individual animal, including :
  • indoor vs outdoor
  • travel plans
  • kennel/boarding plans
  • underlying disease conditions
  • other exposure risks,
  • the disease and vaccine type being considered.
Because these factors may change over time, Blue Ribbon Animal Care recommends routine annual examinations, where a vaccination plan for each individual animal can be decided during a discussion between the veterinarian and animal owner.
