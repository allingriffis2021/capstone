Doctor Alisa Bell

Doctor Bell is our lead Vet and also the founder of Blue Ribbon Animal Care. She graduated from the University of Michigan in 2001 where she obtained a Bachelor's degree in Veterinary Science, Medicine and Surgery (BVMS). Shortly after graduating she moved to the "Traverse City" Area where she opened the Blue Ribbon Animal Care to give the regions pets the top quality and loving attention they deserve.
